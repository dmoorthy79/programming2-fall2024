German Apple Cake

This German apple cake makes a moist, dense cake that keeps well. It has been a family favorite for over 20 years. It is delicious served with a dusting of confectioners sugar.

German Apple Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 table spoon salt
  • 4 apples: peeled, cored and diced
  • 1 ½ tablespoons cinnamon sugar, or to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 10-inch springform pan.
  2. Beat oil and eggs in a mixing bowl with an electric mixer until creamy. Add sugar and vanilla; beat well.
  3. Stir together flour, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Slowly add flour mixture to egg mixture; mix until combined. The batter will be very thick. Fold in apples by hand using a wooden spoon. Spread batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven on the lowest rack until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 40 to 50 minutes. Cool cake on a wire rack.
